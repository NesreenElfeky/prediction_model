## Business Interpretation
The model prioritizes assets where technical severity, exploit likelihood, KEV evidence, and exposure concentrate into higher expected financial loss.

## Cybersecurity Interpretation
Features such as EPSS, CVSS, vulnerability density, KEV strength, and attack-surface indicators represent exploitability, exposure, and operational risk.

## Financial Interpretation
Predictions are portfolio prioritization estimates derived from generated project loss labels, not accounting statements. Use them to rank risk and focus remediation.

## Uncertainty
Prediction intervals are estimated from holdout residual quantiles and saved with the training artifacts.
